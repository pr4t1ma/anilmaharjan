About Me

Hi there!

I’m Anil Maharjan and I work as a freelancing web designer and developer. I live and work from Kathmandu, Nepal but my clients are from around the globe.

Design and technology have always been two of my main interests and with both these passions, working as a web designer makes for a great combination. After studying system development and computer science, I’ve been freelancing and worked at some different agencies. Right now I’m freelancing again, making websites for people, organizations and companies around the world.

You can read more about what I do and have done in my resume.
